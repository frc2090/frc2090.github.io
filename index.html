Team Buff’n Blue 2090 is the robotics team from Punahou School in Honolulu, Hawaii. The team was founded in 2007 and is composed of high school students from the ninth to the twelfth grade. Students work with mentors, coaches and alumni in the fields of electronics, programming, fabrication, computer-aided design and documentation.
