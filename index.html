Team Buff’n Blue 2090 is from Punahou School in Honolulu, Hawaii. The team was founded in 2007 and is composed of high school students from the ninth to twelfth grade. Students work with mentors, coaches and alumni in the fields of electronics, programming, fabrication, CAD and documentation.
